Child Specialist

The mission of the Department of Pediatrics is to improve the health of children and adolescents through excellence in patient care, research and teaching. This mission is embraced by our faculty and staff who are deeply committed to the well-being of our young patients. The Department of Pediatrics provides comprehensive pediatric care and support for the regional referral and consultation centre at Matta Roop rani organ Hospital

We deal with common pediatric ailments and Paediatric Cardiology, Paediatric Oncology, Paediatric Hematology, Paediatric Neurology, Paediatric Surgery, Paediatric Nephrology, Paediatric Endocrinology.

Services Available :

» Nebulisation equipment to relieve respiratory secretions and bronchospasm.

» Latest amenities apart from routine amenities like Central oxygen with its calibrating units, vacuum suction, compressed air units etc.

» Computerized monitors display continuous Electrogram, respiratory rate and graph, Non invasive blood pressure, oxygen saturation with plethysmograph, central venous pressure and arterial blood pressure, transducer recordings, temperature probe

» Mechanical Ventilators

» Infusions pumps.

» D.C. Shock Defibrillators.
